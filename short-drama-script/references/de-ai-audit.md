# De-AI Audit

Use these checks when a script feels too machine-made.

## Structural Checks

- Paragraph length uniformity:
  If too many adjacent paragraphs are almost the same length, rewrite with more varied breathing.
- Hedge density:
  Watch for "似乎", "可能", "或许", "大概", or similar fuzzy phrasing.
- Formulaic transitions:
  Watch for repeated "然而", "不过", "与此同时", "另一方面" patterns.
- Same-prefix sentences:
  Break runs of sentences that begin with the same subject or phrase.

## Language Checks

- Remove analysis-speak:
  Avoid terms that sound like a planning memo instead of lived drama.
- Remove direct emotion labeling:
  Show the hand, face, pause, interruption, or choice.
- Remove over-neat dialogue:
  Let people cut each other off, evade, or answer sideways.

## Rewrite Moves

- Swap explanation for gesture
- Compress long lines
- Add asymmetry to sentence rhythm
- Replace generic intensifiers with physical reaction
- Change at least one line per major character so voices separate more clearly
