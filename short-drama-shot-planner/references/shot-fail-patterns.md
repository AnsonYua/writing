# Shot Fail Patterns

Use this file when shots are technically correct but not attractive enough.

## Common Failures

### Generic staring reaction

Weak:

- character freezes
- character stares
- face is shocked

Why it fails:

- no visible trigger
- no continuity with the next action
- no specific dramatic value

### Generic phone-checking proof shot

Weak:

- she looks at a phone
- he checks the screen

Why it fails:

- proof is not readable
- the panel could belong to any story
- the viewer does not know what matters

### Generic two-person romance frame

Weak:

- two people face each other
- two people stand in the same room

Why it fails:

- no charged spacing
- no asymmetry
- no interruption or blocked contact
- no specific attraction read

### Pretty but empty environment shot

Weak:

- nice room
- good lighting
- tasteful atmosphere

Why it fails:

- no dramatic center
- no pressure
- no hook
- no one-glance takeaway

### Confrontation with unclear power

Weak:

- two people argue
- both occupy the image equally

Why it fails:

- the viewer cannot see who controls the moment
- humiliation or threat is only verbal, not visual

## Recovery Questions

Before keeping a weak panel, ask:

- what does the viewer get in one glance
- where is the payload
- what makes this version hotter than the nearby alternatives
- would this still work as a thumbnail
- does the frame show proof, pressure, attraction, or reversal clearly enough
